PARC: Alignment

Here in the Adobe website we have the alignment of all the pictures and also the text with the same spacing, making this ordered and aligned website easily to navigate.

PARC: Contrast

Here in the apple website I like how they play with the whites and blacks, making the contrast for both parts with the text and background. This is a very elegant and simple way of using contrast.

Visual Hierarchy

Here in the godaddy website, we can see the Hierarchy of the services, from the basic domain to the more detailed information, giving the importance to them to make you watch the product and the price.
