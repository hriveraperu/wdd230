LOCATION

Elio is one of the main neighbourhoods in the Lima Metro area, inside of the Cercado de Lima district. It is between three of the main avenues: Colonial Avenue, Venezuela Avenue and Universitaria Avenue.

INDUSTRY

It has two of the main factories of Lima and Peru country being the first D'onofrio that produces mainly ice cream in Summer and panettone for Christmas, and secondly Molitalia that produces wheat related products and candys. Both companies has a big influence in the Elio area because of their different activities and social awareness.

BUSINESSES

Elio also comprises one of the biggest market places in the area, with more than 100 businesses, thanks to the constant traffic of people and being the cross of Reynaldo Saavedra Avenue and Roberto Thorndike Avenue the authorized places to do business. There are restaurants, pharmacies, beauty saloons, casinos, bazar stores, hardware stores, two markets full of groceries and one branch of one of the most important banks: The Bank of Credit of Peru.

INFLUENCE

Its area of inflence is huge, even the near districts of San Miguel, Breña and the Constitucional Province of Callao. Elio receives visits from students of the University of San Marcos, clients from the Auto fixing area in Chacra Rios (less than 1km away), the employees from industrial areas in Argentina, Colonial and Venezuela Avenue.

GREEN AREA

Finally, Elio recieves a lot of visits from families that come to gather into our 3 parks: Augusto Gonzales park, Francisco de Asis park, and Norma park. All of them recently restored with games for children, giving attraction because of the lack of green area nearby Elio, and making it one of the biggest lungs in the Cercado de Lima district, despite its little area.
